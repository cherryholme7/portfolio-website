Jelly Dodge

Jelly Dodge is my first Android app game. I started developing it in 2014, and finally releasing it in 2016. This app was my sandbox, where I learnt the basics of game and app development. During the development of Jelly Dodge I came accross ocean pollution facts that took me by surprise. I read about the horrifying environmental damages that we Humans cause to the oceans. I took my app development as an opportunity to raise awareness of ocean pollution. While dodging jelly fish, your goal is to help clean up the ocean, one piece of garbage at a time.



Workout Tracker

Workout Tracker is a small and simple workout planner app. Its main functionality is to keep track of your exercises and the progress you make. You may view your progress for each exercise individually, or all of it using a simple calendar graphical user interface.
