About Us
Pappsicle

Pappsicle, founded in 2015 by college students Kevin Cherryholme and Nicolas Beaulieu, is a mobile app game developer inspired to make a healthy change in the world. The passion to embark on a project that results in the global well being of the planet motivates Pappsicle to create and engineer ecstatic games for the public. Learning and practicing from the web has been enough for the Pappsicle CEO's to begin their dream and create their first app named Finny.

Our Story

We are a small group of programmers and designers wanting to create fun and interactive games for the public. Our goal is to entertain our users for as long as possible by providing a chance to challenge friends and to reach high scores. While growing up, we have witnessed a lot of change in our world. For every app purchased, we donate money to charity. Each month, we will select a different charity to donate to in order to help the most people possible. Giving to less fortunate people is a great way to start making a change in the world. Help us make a change by supporting us by having fun with our stimulating apps and games. It takes only one person to make a difference.
